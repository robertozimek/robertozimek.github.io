About

I graduated from Hunter College with a computer science degree in order to purse software development.

Backstory:
It has been my dream to work in the tech field since I was a kid. I had begged my parents to get me my first computer when I was 8 years old, at the time I was more interested in playing video games, but once my parents caved and got me my first Hewlett-Packard computer I had begun to tricker and explore and become fascinated by tech. I spent so many hours in front of my computer, and was unafraid to make any changes to see how things worked (especially after I discovered I can format my computer and reinstall windows if anything ever went wrong 😁).

As I got older and was getting asked that ridiculous question that teachers would ask "what do you wanna be when you grow up?", my 13 year old self didn't really have an answer. I knew I loved tech but hadn't really know about what it was that I really wanted to do. At the time I was still an avid gamer and so I figured I'd like to create video games, but didn't actually know how that was done. So I went online to research and learned all about programming lanugages and software development. And that is when I decided I wanted to purse software development. Since then I have stopped being an avid gaming but I still fully enjoy coding and building software.

Fun Fact:
I speak polish fluently and just started learning german, my goal is to become fluent in the next few years.
